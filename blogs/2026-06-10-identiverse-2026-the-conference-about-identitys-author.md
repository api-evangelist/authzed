AuthZed explores how authorization has become a critical missing layer in identity and access management, particularly as non-human identities and AI agents challenge assumptions that underpinned IAM for two decades. The post announces AuthZed's Identiverse 2026 sponsorship and argues a holistic problem requires purpose-built authorization rather than traditional centralized identity stores.
